F A Q

Welcome to ArtExpo's FAQ section - where we have answered everything you might want to know about our jewelry, ordering process, and services.

1. What makes ArtExpo jewelry special?

With over 30 years of craftsmanship, ArtExpo has created a niche - jewelry perfectly blended between old world charm and modern trends. Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using high-quality materials and timeless design sensibilities - blending tradition with modern elegance.

2. Do you sell both retail and bulk?

Yes! We proudly serve individual customers as well as bulk buyers.

  • Retail: You can shop directly from our website or authorized resellers.
  • Bulk: We have a dedicated division for bulk and export clients, fashion houses, and boutiques. For export inquiries, please contact us at info@artexpo.in.

3. Where are you based? Do you ship internationally?

ArtExpo is based in New Delhi, India. However, we ship worldwide, partnering with reliable international logistics providers to ensure your jewelry reaches you safely and on time.

6. Are your products nickel and lead-free?

Yes. All ArtExpo jewelry is nickel-free and cadmium-free, ensuring it is safe for sensitive skin and compliant with international export regulations.

7. How do I care for my jewelry?

To keep your ArtExpo pieces shining:

  • Store them in a dry, airtight box or pouch.
  • Avoid exposure to perfumes, lotions, or moisture.
  • Gently wipe with a soft cloth after every use.
  • Keep them separate to prevent scratching.
